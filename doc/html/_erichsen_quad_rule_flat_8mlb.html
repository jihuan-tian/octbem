ErichsenQuadRuleFlat - Perform integration on flat panels using the method in Erichsen1996Efficient. The integral is

\[ \int_{\hat{K} \times \hat{K}} H_{loc}(\xi, \eta) k(\xi, \eta) \intd s(\xi) \intd s(\eta), \]

.

where \(H_{loc}(\xi, \eta) = g_K(\xi) g_K(\eta) \hat{\varphi}(\xi) \hat{\psi}(\eta)\) and \(k_{loc}(x, y)\) is the kernel function depending on global coordinates with \(x, y \in \mathbb{R}^3\).

Note: The integration of the product of \(H(x, y)\) and \(k(x, y)\) will be pulled back to the reference cell \(\hat{K}\) first, then to the master cell and will further be pulled back onto the parameter space \((\omega, \eta)\) for the numerical quadrature using Erichsen's method.
